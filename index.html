Update: The link here only shows for the night of Friday June 21st, 2019. You should be able to get the same rate calling in and asking for the "Rosenbaum Wedding" block. We're working on contacting the hotel to fix the link in the mean time.

Unfortunately there's some sort of event going on nearby, so we couldn't get a bigger block. There's plenty of hotels nearby, though, so accommodations shouldn't be a problem if you don't mind being a little spread out. Here's where we'd recommend looking:

Hampton Inn and Suites Baltimore/Woodlawn The Hampton Inn and Suites Baltimore/Woodlawn is conveniently located just off the highway and isn't too far from the wedding venue. It is about a 20 minute drive to Howard County Conservancy (directions).
Sonesta ES Suites Columbia The Sonesta ES Suites Columbia is conveniently located just off the highway and isn't too far from the wedding venue. It is about a 15 minute drive to Howard County Conservancy (directions).
